Questions

Things people ask
before signing up.

Will it actually work with WhatsApp and Signal?
Yes — both WhatsApp and Signal verify accounts by sending a code to a UK mobile number. Your Privify number receives that code and it appears in your inbox within seconds. You then enter it in the app as normal and your account is set up.
Can I have two WhatsApp accounts on one phone?
Yes. WhatsApp supports dual accounts natively on most Android phones, and on iPhone via WhatsApp Business. You just need a second number for the second account — which is exactly what Privify provides.
How long does it take to get my number?
Numbers are typically assigned within 24 hours of signing up. We provision numbers carefully rather than automatically, which means a short wait but a more reliable result. You'll get an email the moment your number is ready.
Can people call the number?
Yes. Inbound calls go to voicemail and the recording is emailed to you as an audio file. You won't miss important calls.
Is the number permanently mine?
Your number is yours for as long as you're subscribed. We don't rotate or share numbers. If you cancel, we hold your number for 30 days before releasing it — so you can come back without losing it.
Will it work for bank OTPs and HMRC?
We provide genuine UK mobile numbers through an established UK telecoms provider, and most major services work well with them. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee — if it doesn't work for your use case, we'll refund you in full.
Can I send texts from the number?
Not currently — Privify is focused on receiving for now. Outbound SMS is on our roadmap. If this matters to you, let us know when you sign up.
Is my data private?
All messages are stored encrypted on UK-based servers. We comply with UK GDPR, don't sell your data, and don't serve ads. You can delete your account and all data at any time from your settings.
