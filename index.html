Questions

Things people ask
before signing up.

How does email forwarding work? +
Every text that arrives on your Privify number is forwarded to the email address on your account. The email shows who sent it and includes the full message. There is nothing to configure — it works as soon as your number is active.
Can I change the forwarding email? +
Yes — update it any time from account settings. Changes take effect immediately.
What's the setup fee for? +
The £12 one-time setup fee covers provisioning your dedicated UK mobile number. Charged once when your number is activated. Your £5.99/month covers everything else.
How quickly will I get my number? +
Numbers are typically assigned within 24 hours of signing up. We set each one up individually to make sure everything works correctly.
Will it work for bank OTPs and HMRC? +
We provide genuine UK mobile numbers through an established UK telecoms provider, and most major services including banks and HMRC work well with them. You have a 14-day statutory right to cancel — if it doesn't work for your use case, contact us at support@privify.co.uk.
Can people call the number? +
Yes, people can call the number. All inbound calls go to voicemail and you cannot answer live. You get the recording as an audio file by email, plus an automatic transcription so you can read the message without listening to it.
Is the number permanently mine? +
Your number is yours for as long as you're subscribed. We don't rotate or share numbers. If you cancel, your number is released — if you want to keep it long-term, keep the subscription active.
Can I send texts from the number? +
Not at the moment. Privify is built for receiving. Outbound SMS is something we are working on. If it matters to you, mention it when you sign up.
Is my data private and secure? +
All messages and voicemails are stored on servers within the European Union. We comply with UK and EU GDPR, don't sell your data, and don't serve ads. Voicemail transcription runs entirely on our own EU infrastructure. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I port my existing number? +
Yes — if your number is still active, you can port it to Privify and keep receiving texts on it indefinitely. Text PAC to 65075 to get your PAC code. Networks are legally required to provide it within 2 hours, free of charge. Ports typically complete within one working day. Standard £12 setup fee applies.
What if I've already cancelled my old number? +
If you cancelled within the last month it may still be possible. Get in touch and we will look into it. If it has been longer than that the number has likely been released and cannot be recovered. The best time to port is always before you cancel your old contract.
Where is my data stored? +
All infrastructure — messages, voicemails, transcripts, account data — runs on servers within the European Union. Your data never leaves the EU. Voicemail transcription runs on our own servers too.
